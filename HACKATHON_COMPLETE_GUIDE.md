## 🎤 Delivery Tips (Most Important)

### Opening Hook (30 seconds - CRITICAL)

**❌ Don't:**
"Hi, we're Shubham and Shravani, and we built a support automation system for EdTech..."

**✅ Do:**
"How many of you took an online course and waited 2+ days for a support response?
 What if I told you we solve that in 5 minutes?"

**Why:** Emotional connection. They relate immediately.

### Problem Statement (Most Important Slide)

**Tell a story:**
"Priya enrolls in an MBA. She gets an error at checkout. She emails support.
 She waits 24 hours. Still waiting. She refunds. She's gone.
 This happens 1,000 times a day in EdTech."

**Why:** Stories stick. Judges FEEL it.

### Solution (Keep it Simple)

"Five seconds.
 Email comes in. AI reads it. Knows it's a Finance problem. Creates a ticket.
 Agent sees it. Responds in 15 minutes.
 That's the entire system."

**Why:** Simple > complex. They get it immediately.

### Closing (Inspirational)

"250 million students are waiting for support right now.
 We're building the system that changes that.
 Join us. Let's go."

**Why:** Emotional. Memorable. They'll think about it after.
